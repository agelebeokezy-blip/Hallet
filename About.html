Nestled in the heart of Orogun Delta, DE HALLET stands as the city's premier multi experience destination where sophistication meets unstoppable energy, and every visit feels like stepping into your own private world of indulgence. We didn't build just another lounge. We created a complete lifestyle escape under one expansive, beautifully designed roof. Whether you're chasing daytime relaxation or midnight euphoria, DE HALLET delivers it seamlessly and in style.

Start your day (or afternoon) with a refreshing dip in our sparkling swimming pool, surrounded by lush loungers, chilled cocktails, and panoramic views that make every moment Instagram worthy. Craving friendly competition? Head to our high energy game area think premium pool tables, arcade classics, interactive darts, and more perfect for groups, dates, or spontaneous showdowns with friends. When hunger calls, our multiple restaurants serve up the best of both worlds: authentic local Naija flavors bursting with jollof mastery, sizzling suya, pepper soup perfection, and regional specialties prepared with love and precision alongside elevated continental dishes ranging from juicy steaks and fresh seafood to gourmet pastas, fusion creations, and internationally inspired plates that satisfy every palate.

As evening turns to night, the atmosphere transforms. Our state of the art nightclub ignites with world class sound systems, electrifying DJ lineups spinning Afrobeat, Amapiano, hip-hop, house, and global hits. The dance floor pulses, premium bottle service flows, and the crowd brings that signature Delta flair elegant, unapologetic, unforgettable. From golden-hour poolside vibes to late night celebrations, from family brunches and corporate mixers to epic birthday bashes and romantic evenings, DE HALLET is engineered for every mood, every occasion, every chapter of your story.

Pool Area

Discover the ultimate urban oasis at our signature pool, nestled within the heart of the lounge. Surrounded by sleek loungers, whether you’re gliding through the water, sipping a signature cocktail from the poolside bar, or unwinding on a plush daybed with your favorite playlist in the background, every moment feels effortlessly luxurious.

By day, soak up the sun and enjoy light bites and chilled drinks. As evening sets in, the space transforms with underwater lighting, glowing fire pits, and a vibrant yet relaxed vibe—perfect for intimate conversations, small celebrations, or simply lounging in style.

Exquisite Restaurant

Step into a world of refined flavors and effortless elegance at one of our signature restaurant, this is where culinary excellence meets sophisticated ambiance in perfect harmony. Our beautifully designed dining space blends modern luxury with warm, intimate vibes.

Whether you’re seated in the sleek indoor area with its dramatic lighting and plush seating, or enjoying the art, every table offers an unforgettable experience.

Our menu celebrates the best of contemporary fusion cuisine — thoughtfully crafted dishes that range from fresh seafood and premium grilled meats to vibrant vegetarian and plant-based creations. Every plate is prepared with the finest ingredients and paired perfectly with an impressive selection of wines, signature cocktails, and refreshing mocktails

Mini Football Pitch

Unwind, compete, and have fun at our premium mini football pitch — the perfect blend of sport, style, and social energy right within the Lounge.

This beautifully lit, all-weather 5-a-side pitch is crafted with premium artificial turf for a smooth, professional playing experience. Whether you’re a serious player looking to sharpen your skills or simply want to enjoy a casual game with friends, our pitch delivers excitement in a sophisticated setting.

Surrounded by comfortable spectator seating, ambient lighting, and close to the pool and restaurant, it’s the ultimate social sports destination. Day or night, the energy is electric yet relaxed — perfect for friendly matches, corporate team-building, birthday celebrations, or just blowing off steam after work.

Lounge Games Area

Elevate your evenings with friendly competition and sophisticated fun at our exclusive Games Area, designed as a stylish social hub, this space perfectly blends classic elegance with modern leisure, making it the ideal spot to unwind, challenge friends, or break the ice.

Step inside to discover two premium experiences: a full-size professional snooker table and high-quality table tennis setup. Whether you prefer the strategic, calm focus of snooker or the fast-paced, energetic rallies of table tennis, the atmosphere is always relaxed yet vibrant.

Soft ambient lighting, plush seating, and a dedicated bar service create the perfect backdrop for hours of entertainment. The area seamlessly connects to the pool and restaurant, so you can enjoy drinks and light bites while playing.
